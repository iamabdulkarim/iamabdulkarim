## 🚀 About Me

I am a **Senior Software Engineer** with **5+ years of experience** building **scalable, secure, and high-performance web applications** and backend systems.

I currently work at **Siscotek** as a **Senior Software Engineer**, where I contribute to large-scale business platforms including **B2B e-commerce systems** and **U.S.-based property management systems** such as **Gaag2** and **PMSv2**.

Previously, I worked as a **Senior Software Engineer at CliqPack**, where I led backend and API-focused development for enterprise applications, microservices, and cloud-based systems serving high-volume workloads. My experience includes designing production-ready systems, optimizing performance, integrating third-party platforms, and improving deployment reliability.

I specialize in:

- **Microservices and modular backend architecture**
- **Database optimization, caching, and performance tuning**
